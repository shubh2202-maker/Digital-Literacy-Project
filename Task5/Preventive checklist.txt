Stay Safe Online Checklist:

* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts

* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

* Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails

* Never share personal information like passwords or OTPs

* Use only official apps and websites for transactions

* Avoid using public WiFi for banking activities

* Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

* Never share your UPI PIN with anyone

* Always verify payment requests before accepting

* Report cybercrime at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in

* Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
